Some words on Jung. Grandfather of depth psychology.

Carl Jung dedicated much of his life to understanding the subconscious realms, seeking meaning and patterns in the chaos. He believed there are stories and archetypes that we share, he called this the 'collective unconscious'. Jung obviously did not have access to data in the same way we do today, I often wonder what he would have discovered if he was able to work with the tools we have today.
